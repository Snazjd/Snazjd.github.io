About Me

Hi there. My name is Jonathan (Jack) Reynolds. I am a mechanical engineer with a BSME from Purdue University, specializing in CAD modeling, rapid prototyping, and mechanical drafting. My experience spans academic projects, professional work, and personal engineering endeavors, with a focus on aerospace and robotics.

I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments where clear communication and teamwork drive innovation. Whether refining designs for manufacturability or iterating prototypes to solve real-world challenges, I bring precision and creativity to every project.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy playing tabletop and board games, following Formula 1, golfing, and watching college basketball.
